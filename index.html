Who am I?


I'm an ambitious Developer from Bad Reichenhall who pays attention to the little details and loves learning. I wrote my first lines of code in 2019 on CodeCademy.

In January 2022 i finally took the leap and joined the Web & App Development Bootcamp by WBS Coding School. The approach here was project based learning. Every week we had 2 lectures about new subjects and every day we had group work to implement the things we have learned into 1 - 2 week long projects.At the end of the week we had to present our work to the teacher and the other students. After 15 life-changing weeks of intense learning, i finished the bootcamp.

The last 3 weeks i was at the WBS Campus in Berlin. With 3 other students i worked on a final project, a yoga lesson creator, which we then presented to a jury and a large audience at the campus and at home. I'm amazed at how much i was able to learn in such a short amount time but that was only the beginning of my journey.
